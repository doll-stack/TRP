About Us

The Rhythm Project (TRP) is more than just a band—we're a musical experience. Bringing together the soulful depths of Sufi fusion, the vibrant energy of Bollywood, and timeless covers that resonate with every generation.


Our journey began with a simple belief: music transcends boundaries. Whether it's the mystical melodies of Sufi traditions, the foot-tapping rhythms of Bollywood classics, or reimagined covers of global hits, we pour our hearts into every performance.


From intimate venues to grand stages, TRP has captivated audiences across the country. Our sound is unique, our energy infectious, and our passion unmatched. Join us on this sonic adventure and feel the rhythm that connects us all.
